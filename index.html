Special City

DIY is known as a simple city, friendly, arts and culture, as well as natural beauty

Jomblang Cave

Jomblang Cave was formed thousands of years ago when a 300 meter piece of land fell into a sinkhole. This place is located on Jl. Ireda No. 65/139, Keparakan, Kec. Mergangsan, City of Yogyakarta, Special Region of Yogyakarta 55152

Klangon

Kelangon Hill is one of the highest places in Yogyakarta. the distance from the crater to the hill is about 4 kilometers. located in Kalitengah Lor, Glagaharjo, Cangkringan, Sleman Regency, Special Region of Yogyakarta 55583.

Prambanan Temple

andi Prambanan is the largest Hindu temple in Indonesia. This temple was built around the mid-9th century by the king of the Sanjaya dynasty, namely Raja Balitung Maha Sambu. Located Jl. Raya Solo - Yogyakarta No. 16, Kranggan, Bokoharjo, Kec. Prambanan, Sleman Regency, Special Region of Yogyakarta 55571.

Curug Pulosari

Pulosari waterfall is usually used for pre-wedding shooting, shooting for case studies or comparative studies, because in this place visitors will find the true beauty of nature. Located in Krebet, Sendangsari, Pajangan, Krebet, Sendangsari, Kec. Bantul, Bantul Regency, Special Region of Yogyakarta 55751.
